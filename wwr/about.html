Whitewater Rafting

At Whitewater Rafting our mission is to provide premier whitewater rafting experiences. We pride ourselves on making your time with us safe, enjoyable, and educational. We are committed to operational excellence for our customers, our community, and our natural environment.

History

Throughout the 1980’s, the Williams family consisting of James and Elizabeth Williams with their sons James Jr., Sam, Bill “Boomer”, and Peter, spent two weeks each summer rafting the Snake and Colorado rivers. As the boys grew, they spent as much time as they could seeking great whitewater throughout the Intermountain West. In 1998, Boomer Williams decided to take his passion for whitewater rafting and share it with the world. Whitewater Rafting was born.

For over 25 years, Whitewater Rafting has been providing high-quality rafting expeditions on some of the world’s most beautiful and thrilling whitewater in Utah and Colorado. We have grown from a small family operation of four enthusiastic young men to a staff of 40 exceptionally skilled and qualified individuals. Each expedition shows our passion for enjoying nature to its fullest. Come join us and feel the thrill that only can be found on free-moving whitewater.
